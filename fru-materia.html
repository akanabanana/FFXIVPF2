TLDR

  1. Your position is dependent on the first icicle that appears within waymark 1A2B.
  2. Each player is assigned a quadrant position relative to this first icicle. You are only changing position in that quadrant (ie, if your quadrant is the West to North quadrant, you’re only moving positions around this quadrant)
  3. Once Axe kick/Scythe kick cast appears, go to your positions to bait/place puddle
  4. Your assigned KB groups is also relative to the first icicle. LP1 (MTH1D1D3) is relative north and LP2 (STH2D2D4) is relative South
